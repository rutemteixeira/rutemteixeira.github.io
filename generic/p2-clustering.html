Project ID: Client Segmentation for Delivery Platform

Duration

5 weeks (2024)

Industry

B2C - Food and Beverage (e-commerce)

Dataset

30,000+ rows, 52 columns
3 months of purchase history

Collaboration

Team Work

Skillset

Pandas, Python, Scikit-learn, Clustering Alorithms, RFM Analsis, EDA

Data Source

Public

Project Scope

Customer segmentation for a B2C food delivery platform, analyzing both behavior (ordering patterns, purchase frequency) and preferences (cuisine types, spending habits) to develop hyper-targeted marketing strategies, incorporating at least two distinct segmentation perspectives.

Key Challenges

  • Data Skewness and outliers required log transformations and iterative outlier removal
  • Cluster interpretability
  • Model selection balancing all tradeoffs

Technical Approach

Phase Techniques
Data Preparation KNN imputation, log transformation
Feature Engineering RFM metrics, time-based bins, aggregations of data
Model Selection Self-Organizing Maps (SOM's) merged with Hierarchical Clustering
Evaluation R-Squared, Silhouette/Inertia, AIC/BIC, t-SNE visualziations

Results

~94% R-Squared

Very close to 1, the predictors explain very well the variance in the dependent variable

Key Observations and Future Improvements

  • Correctly identifying duplicates requires a good understanding of how customer are registered on database
