About Me

My name is Rute Teixeira, I just finished my first year of Master's Degree in Data Science and Advanced Analytics with a specialization in Business Analytics at Nova IMS, in Lisbon, Portugal.

As Data Science is quite a vast and dynamic field, my passions and interests are scattered around a few niches.
